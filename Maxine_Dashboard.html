📖 Toddler nutrition principles — click to expand ▾
  • Division of Responsibility (Satter): adult decides what / when / where, child decides whether / how much. No pressure, no praise for eating.
  • Iron-rich foods daily (lentils, beef, fortified cereal). Calcium via dairy or alternatives.
  • Limit added sugars. Whole fruits over juice. Water as default drink.
  • Brave-bite: offer new foods 10–15 times without pressure. Don't force.
  • Family meals together when possible — biggest predictor of food acceptance.
